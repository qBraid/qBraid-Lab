qbraid envs
============

Manage qBraid environments.

Commands
---------
+---------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------+
| ``qbraid envs list``                  | Get list of installed qBraid environments.        |
+---------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------+
| ``qbraid envs activate``              | Activate qBraid environment.                      |
+---------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------+
| ``qbraid envs uninstall``             | Uninstall qBraid environment.                     |
+---------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------+
